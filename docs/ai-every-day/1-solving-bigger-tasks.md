# Solving Bigger Tasks

AI can help with more than just one question.

You can use it step by step to solve a full task.

---

## Think in Steps

Instead of asking one big question, break it into smaller steps.

👉 Ask → Review → Improve → Continue

---

## Example: Planning Meals

Step 1:

> Create a healthy meal plan for one week

Step 2:

> Create a shopping list from this plan

Step 3:

> Make this cheaper

Step 4:

> Adjust for vegetarian diet

---

## Example: Writing a Letter

Step 1:

> Write a complaint about a delayed delivery

Step 2:

> Make it more polite

Step 3:

> Shorten the message

---

## Example: Planning a Trip

Step 1:

> Plan a day trip near Loch Lomond

Step 2:

> Make it suitable for someone aged 70

Step 3:

> Suggest places to eat

---

## Why This Helps

- Saves time  
- Breaks down complex tasks  
- Gives better, more useful results  

---

## Try It Yourself

Think of something you want to do.

For example:
- Plan something  
- Write something  
- Organize something  

Now:

1. Ask AI to help  
2. Improve the answer  
3. Ask the next step  

---

## Key Message

👉 You can work with AI step by step to solve bigger problems.
