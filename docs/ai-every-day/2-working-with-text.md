# Working with Your Own Text

AI can help you understand and improve your own text.

You can copy and paste text into AI and ask for help.

---

## What You Can Do

You can ask AI to:

- Explain something in simple words  
- Rewrite text to sound more polite  
- Shorten long messages  
- Fix spelling and grammar  
- Summarize information  

---

## Example: Understanding a Letter

Paste the text and ask:

> Explain this in simple terms

👉 Helpful for letters, emails, or official messages

---

## Example: Writing a Message

Paste your draft and ask:

> Can you make this more polite?

or

> Can you make this clearer?

---

## Example: Shortening Text

Paste text and ask:

> Can you summarize this?

👉 Useful for long emails or documents

---

## Example: Improving Writing

Paste your message and ask:

> Can you fix spelling and grammar?

---

## Important Tips

- Only share text you are comfortable sharing  
- Remove personal or sensitive information if needed  
- Always read the result before using it  

---

## Try It Yourself

Think of something you already have:

- A message you want to send  
- A letter you received  
- Notes or text you wrote  

Paste it into AI and ask for help.

---

## Key Message

👉 AI can help you understand and improve your own writing.
