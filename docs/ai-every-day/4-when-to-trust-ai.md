# When to Trust AI

AI is helpful, but it’s important to know when to trust it.

---

## Good Uses for AI

AI is usually safe to use for:

- Writing emails or messages  
- Getting ideas or suggestions  
- Explaining things in simple terms  
- Planning (meals, trips, daily tasks)  

👉 These are low-risk tasks.

---

## Be Careful With

Be more careful when using AI for:

- Health advice  
- Financial decisions  
- Legal matters  
- Important personal decisions  

👉 Always double-check this type of information.

---

## Use AI as a Starting Point

Think of AI as:

👉 A first draft  
👉 A helper  

Not the final answer.

---

## Check Important Information

If something matters, you should:

- Check another source  
- Ask a professional if needed  
- Use trusted websites (like NHS)  

---

## Trust Your Judgement

If something doesn’t feel right:

👉 Question it  
👉 Ask again  
👉 Check elsewhere  

---

## Example

AI can help you write a message to your GP.

But it should not replace medical advice.

---

## Simple Rule

👉 Use AI for help and ideas  
👉 Not for final decisions on important matters  

---

## Key Message

👉 AI is a useful tool — but you should always stay in control.
