# Understanding Tabs

## What Is a Tab?

A **tab** is like a page in a notebook.

Each tab lets you open a different website without closing the one you are already using.

This means you can move between several websites easily.

For example, you might have tabs open for:

- The weather forecast
- Email
- Online shopping
- News

Tabs appear at the **top of your browser window**.

---

## Opening a New Tab

Most browsers show a **+ button** next to the existing tabs.

Clicking this will open a new empty tab.


---

## Switching Between Tabs

To move between tabs:

1. Look at the row of tabs at the top of your browser.
2. Click the tab you want to view.

The active tab is usually **highlighted**.

---

## Closing a Tab

Each tab has a small **X** on it.

Clicking the **X** closes that tab.

You can also close the current tab with:

**Ctrl + W** (Windows)  
**Command + W** (Mac)

Do not worry — closing a tab does **not** close your whole browser.

---

## Why Tabs Are Useful

Tabs allow you to:

- Compare information on different websites
- Keep several things open at once
- Return to something later without searching again

They make browsing the internet much easier.

---

## When You Have Too Many Tabs

It is very common to accidentally open many tabs.

This can:

- Make the browser harder to navigate
- Slow down older computers
- Make it difficult to find the page you want

If this happens, simply close the tabs you no longer need.

---

:::tip Did you know?
Many people accidentally open **dozens of tabs** without realising it.

If your browser looks crowded at the top, it may be time to close a few!
:::

---

## Try This

1. Open your browser.
2. Click the **+ button** to open a new tab.
3. Search for something interesting.
4. Open another tab and search for something else.
5. Switch between them.

You are now using tabs!

---

## Common Confusion

• Closing a tab does **not** close your browser.  
• Tabs are different from opening a whole new browser window.  
• You can have many tabs open at once.

---

## Key Takeaway

Tabs allow you to open and switch between multiple websites inside the same browser window.
