# Try It Yourself

This page contains simple step-by-step guides to help you enable accessibility tools on your device.

You can try these tools during a session or later at home.

Accessibility tools can help with:

- making text larger
- reading text aloud
- improving colour contrast
- navigating websites more easily

Choose the guide that matches your device or the tool you want to try.  

## Make Text Larger on Windows

1. Click the **Start button**.
2. Click **Settings**.
3. Select **Accessibility**.
4. Choose **Text Size**.
5. Move the **slider** to increase the size of the text.
6. Click **Apply**.

Your computer will increase the text size across many apps and menus.

## Use Reader Mode in Your Browser

Reader Mode removes adverts and distractions from webpages.

1. Open a news article or blog post.
2. Look for the **Reader Mode icon** in the address bar.
3. Click the icon.

The page will switch to a cleaner layout that is easier to read.

## Use Text-to-Speech in the Browser

Some browsers can read text aloud.

1. Highlight the text you want to hear.
2. Right-click the highlighted text.
3. Choose **Read Aloud** or **Speak**.

Your computer will begin reading the text aloud.
