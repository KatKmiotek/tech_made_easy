# Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility features make computers and websites easier to use for everyone.

They are especially helpful for people who:

- have difficulty seeing small text
- struggle to read long pages
- have limited mobility
- find technology confusing

Many modern devices include built-in tools that make websites easier to read and navigate.

---

## Examples of Accessibility Tools

Accessibility tools can help in many ways.

For example:

- Making text larger
- Reading text aloud
- Improving colour contrast
- Simplifying page layout

Many of these tools are already built into your computer, tablet, or phone.

---

:::tip Did you know?
Most modern computers and smartphones include powerful accessibility tools, but many people never realize they are available.
:::

---

## Accessibility Helps Everyone

Accessibility tools are not only for people with disabilities.

They can also help when:

- you are tired
- text is too small
- you are reading a long article
- English is not your first language

---

## Your Right to Accessible Websites

In the UK, many organizations are required to make their websites accessible.

This includes many:

- government services
- NHS websites
- local councils
- public organisations

If a website is difficult to use with assistive technology, you have the right to report the problem.

Accessibility helps make the internet usable for everyone.
