# AI Safety

AI is helpful, but it’s important to use it safely.

---

## Do Not Share Personal Information

Never share:
- Passwords
- Bank details
- Personal or private information

---

## AI Can Be Wrong

AI does not always give correct answers.

Always:
- Double-check important information
- Be careful with health or financial advice

---

## Be Aware of Scams

AI can be used by scammers.

Be cautious if:
- Something sounds too good to be true
- You are asked for money or personal details

---

## Think of AI as a Tool

AI is there to help, but:

👉 You are always in control  
👉 Use your judgement  

---

## Key Message

👉 AI is useful — but always use it carefully.
