# Practice with AI

Let’s practice using AI together.

You can work on your own or with the person next to you.

---

## Exercise 1: Make It Better

Start with a simple question:

> Help with email

Now improve it:

> Write a friendly email asking my neighbor to return my ladder

Compare the answers.

👉 What changed? Was the second one more useful?

---

## Exercise 2: Real Life Problem

Think of something from your daily life.

For example:
- Writing a message or complaint  
- Understanding a letter  
- Planning a meal or trip  

Ask AI for help.

If you are working with someone:
- Discuss what to ask  
- Look at the answer together  

👉 Did it help solve your problem?

---

## Exercise 3: Improve the Answer

Ask AI something simple.

Then try:

- "Can you make this simpler?"
- "Can you make it shorter?"
- "Can you make it more friendly?"

👉 See how the answer changes.

---

## Take Your Time

There is no rush.

- Try different questions  
- Explore what works  
- Ask for help if you need it  

---

## Key Message

👉 The more you use AI, the more confident you will become.
