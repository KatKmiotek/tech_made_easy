# Using AI Effectively

You can get better results from AI by asking better questions.

---

## Be Clear

Instead of:

> Help with email

Try:

> Write a friendly email asking my neighbor to return my ladder

---

## Add Details

The more detail you give, the better the answer.

Example:

> Plan a healthy weekly meal plan for one person

---

## Ask for Style or Tone

You can ask AI to change how it writes.

Examples:

- "Make it simple"
- "Make it shorter"
- "Make it more formal"
- "Make it friendly"

---

## Ask Follow-Up Questions

You don’t need to start again.

You can say:

- "Can you explain that more simply?"
- "Can you shorten this?"
- "Can you give another example?"

---

## Try Improving a Question

Take a simple question and improve it.

Example:

Before:

> Help with shopping

After:

> Create a simple shopping list for healthy meals for one week

---

## Key Message

👉 The better your question, the better the answer.
